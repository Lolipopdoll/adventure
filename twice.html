ABOUT TWICE

Twice is a South Korean girl group that has captured the hearts of fans all around the world with their charming music, adorable personalities, and infectious energy. The group consists of nine talented members: Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Momo, Sana, Jihyo, Mina, Dahyun, Chaeyoung, and Tzuyu. Twice is known for their catchy pop music, which often features upbeat melodies and fun choreography that fans can't help but dance along to. Their music videos are visually stunning and showcase the group's lively personalities, making them a delight to watch. In addition to their music, Twice is also beloved for their positive messages of self-confidence and unity, which have resonated with fans around the world. They are a true force of positivity in the entertainment industry and have inspired countless fans to be themselves and pursue their dreams. With their incredible talent, infectious energy, and endearing personalities, Twice has become a beloved group in the K-pop scene and beyond. Whether you're a longtime fan or a newcomer, their music and message are sure to brighten your day!
