ABOUT TXT

TXT (Tomorrow X Together) is a K-pop boy group under Big Hit Music that has quickly become a fan favorite. Comprised of members Soobin, Yeonjun, Beomgyu, Taehyun, and Huening Kai, the group debuted in 2019 and has since gained a massive following with their unique sound, impressive dance skills, and captivating performances. TXT's music is a blend of various genres, including pop, hip-hop, and rock, resulting in a fresh and distinct sound that sets them apart from other groups. Their lyrics often focus on personal growth and self-reflection, making their music relatable and inspiring for fans of all ages. In addition to their music, TXT is also known for their striking visuals and fashion-forward style, with each member bringing their own unique flair to the group's overall aesthetic. As a Big Hit group, TXT has already achieved significant success in the industry, earning numerous awards and nominations for their music and performances. Their passion, dedication, and talent have made them a rising star in the K-pop scene and one to watch in the years to come.
